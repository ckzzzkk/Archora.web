Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  ·  Last updated: March 2026

ASORIA ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ASORIA mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

By using the App you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address (used for sign-in and communication)
  • Display name and avatar image (set by you, optional)

App content you create

  • Architectural blueprints, room layouts, and design projects
  • Furniture placements and material selections
  • AR scan session data (used to generate your room layout; stored securely)
  • Exported renders and thumbnail images
  • Templates you publish to the community feed
  • Comments, likes, ratings, and saves on community templates

AI prompt data

  • Text prompts you type or speak to generate buildings, furniture, or textures
  • These are sent to third-party AI providers (listed below) to fulfil your request. We do not use your prompts to train our own models.

Subscription and billing

  • Your subscription tier (Starter, Creator, or Architect)
  • Payment is processed entirely by Stripe. ASORIA never sees or stores your card details.

Engagement data

  • Points total and streak count (for in-app rewards)
  • Daily editing duration (Starter tier only, to enforce the plan limit)

Device sensors (never transmitted)

  • Accelerometer / gyroscope readings are used on-device only: to detect a shake gesture (undo/redo) and to tilt the 3D view. This data is never stored or sent to our servers.

Local storage

  • Blueprint data is cached locally on your device using MMKV for offline access.
  • Your authentication token (JWT) is stored in the device's secure enclave (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To save, sync, and display your design projects across devices
  • To process your AI generation requests
  • To operate the community template marketplace
  • To manage your subscription and process payments via Stripe
  • To enforce plan limits (projects, rooms, AI quota, editing time)
  • To calculate points and streaks for in-app engagement
  • To send account-related notifications (e.g. comments on your templates)
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the App

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not serve advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following sub-processors only as necessary to operate the App. Each has its own privacy policy linked below.

4. Device Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera — used for AR room scanning. Camera images are processed on-device and used only to construct a room layout.
  • Microphone — used for the voice-to-prompt feature. Audio is sent to OpenAI Whisper for transcription and then discarded.
  • Photo library — used to save exported design renders to your device's photo library. We do not read or access existing photos.

5. Data Retention

  • Your account and all associated data are retained for as long as your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, your personal data and projects are permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Published community templates may remain in anonymised form for a short period to preserve data integrity for other users; these are also deleted on request.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your account and personal data
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection / restriction — object to or restrict certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@asoria.app. We will respond within 30 days.

7. California Residents (CCPA)

We do not sell personal information. California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of personal information (which we do not engage in). To exercise these rights, contact us at support@asoria.app.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures: all data in transit is encrypted via TLS, data at rest is encrypted by Supabase (AES-256), authentication tokens are stored in the device's secure enclave, and Row-Level Security policies ensure users can only access their own data.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@asoria.app
